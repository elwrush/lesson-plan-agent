FLUENCY SECRET

WORD PARTNERS

English words have 'best friends'. We call these Collocations.

Don't translate word-for-word. Learn which words go together (like 'Rough Sea') to sound natural.

Explain the concept of collocations.

ACCURACY

PRECISION TRAINING

B1/B2 exams often trick you with words that look similar but have different meanings.

Let's spot the difference between commonly confused words.

Warn them about distractors and similar words.

TASK 4: WORD CHOICE

These words are often confused. Choosing the right one gets you higher marks.
1The beach was lovely / calm / sandy / dirty
2We went for a drive / shop / drink / stroll
3The sea was sandy / calm / rough / cold
Ask 'Why?' for each one. E.g. You can't go for a 'shop', you go 'shopping'.
