### Slide 27-32: MISSION 3 ANSWERS (Interleaved)
- **Visual**: Teal Background (#052a10)
- **Formula**: [Term] + [Definition/Explanation]
- *Answer 1*: **self-defense**: An action taken specifically to protect oneself or others from danger.
- *Answer 2*: **fight or flight instinct**: The body's natural "auto-pilot" that helps us survive by either facing a threat or escaping.
- *Answer 3*: **physical abilities**: Gross motor skills like sprinting or jumping that significantly improve under stress.
- *Answer 4*: **adrenaline**: A powerful chemical messenger (hormone) that triggers a sudden surge of physical energy.
- *Answer 5*: **glucose**: A specific type of sugar processed by the body to provide "fuel" for our muscles.
- *Answer 6*: **organs**: Vital internal parts of the body, like the heart and lungs, that perform essential jobs to keep us alive.
