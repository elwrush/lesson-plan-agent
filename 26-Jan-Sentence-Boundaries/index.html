**Advice**: We are defining the 'Three Crimes' of sentence boundaries. **Next**: Mystery 1.
DEFINITION

1. THE COMMA SPLICE

A comma is too weak to hold two sentences.

❌ I am tired, I go to bed.

✅ I am tired. I go to bed.

**Advice**: Use the visual of the string breaking. A comma cannot support two full thoughts. **Next**: Mystery 2.
DEFINITION

2. THE FUSED SENTENCE

No wall at all! Thoughts crash.

❌ I am tired I go to bed.

✅ I am tired. I go to bed.

**Advice**: A car crash. Two facts need a full stop as a wall between them. **Next**: Mystery 3.
DEFINITION

3. THE INFINITE FLOW

Going on forever.

❌ ...because I am tired and because...

✅ One 'because' is okay. Two is illegal.

**Advice**: Remind students that in Thai, thoughts flow. In English, we need to stop and start. **Next**: Task 1 Segue.
