## Answer Keys: The 7 Repairs (Interleaved)
*Note: Every answer slide uses Auto-Animate to highlight the correction.*

### Slide 16/17: Answer 1 (Auto-Animate)
- **Slide A**: I am so tired I want to go to bed.
- **Slide B**: I am so tired. **I** want to go to bed.
- **Explanation**: These are two separate facts. They need a full stop and a big letter gateway.

### Slide 18/19: Answer 2 (Auto-Animate)
- **Slide A**: She is very kind, everyone likes her.
- **Slide B**: She is very kind. **E**veryone likes her.
- **Explanation**: Don't use a comma to link two sentences. Use a full stop.

### Slide 20/21: Answer 3 (Auto-Animate)
- **Slide A**: My friend lives in London, mean while I live in Bangkok.
- **Slide B**: My friend lives in London. **M**eanwhile, I live in Bangkok.
- **Explanation**: 'Meanwhile' is a transition. It starts a new sentence.

### Slide 22/23: Answer 4 (Auto-Animate)
- **Slide A**: We went to the mall we bought some new shoes for the party.
- **Slide B**: We went to the mall. **W**e bought some new shoes for the party.
- **Explanation**: New action, new sentence!

### Slide 24/25: Answer 5 (Auto-Animate)
- **Slide A**: ...because fear can stop us... because I think...
- **Slide B**: ...because fear can stop us. **I**t makes us feel small...
- **Explanation**: Split the sentence at the second 'because' to help the reader breathe.

### Slide 26/27: Answer 6 (Auto-Animate)
- **Slide A**: The movie was extremely long, therefore many people left...
- **Slide B**: The movie was extremely long. **T**herefore, many people left...
- **Explanation**: 'Therefore' is a heavy connector. It Needs a full stop before it.

### Slide 28/29: Answer 7 (Auto-Animate)
- **Slide A**: Success takes risk because without risk you cannot grow, failure is just a lesson.
- **Slide B**: Success takes risk. **W**ithout risk you cannot grow. **F**ailure is just a lesson.
- **Explanation**: Three distinct points! Each one needs its own "Gateway."
