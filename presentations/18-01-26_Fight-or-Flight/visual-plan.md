# Visual Plan: Fight or Flight - Adrenaline/Primal Theme

## Theme Definition
- **Theme**: Adrenaline / Primal (High Energy, Survival)
- **Colors**:
  - Background: `#0A0A0A` (Deep Black)
  - Accent 1: `#A62D26` (Bell Maroon - "Blood/Urgency")
  - Accent 2: `#FFC107` (Amber - "Warning/Instinct")
  - Text: `#FFFFFF` (White - High Contrast)
- **Motifs**: Heartbeat/EKG lines, "Warning" stripes, Silhouettes of motion/running.

---

## Slide Structure

### Slide 1: Title (The Hook)
- **Visual**: A high-contrast image of eyes wide open (fear response) or a silhouette of someone sprinting. Overlay with a subtle EKG heartbeat animation.
- **Text**: "FIGHT OR FLIGHT" (Huge, Impact font) + "The Biology of Fear" (Subtext).
- **Audio**: (Optional) Heartbeat sound effect.

### Slide 2: Segue - STAGE 1
- **Background**: Solid Maroon (`#A62D26`) with Amber warning stripes at the bottom.
- **Text**: "STAGE 1: THE SIGNAL" (or "LEAD-IN / ACTIVATION") - "What happens when you are scared?" (White text).

### Slide 3: Concept Check (Engagement)
- **Visual**: Split screen. Left: "Calm". Right: "PANIC".
- **Interaction**: "Symtoms Check" list appearing one by one:
  1. Heart Beating Fast (Icon: Heart)
  2. Breathing Quickly (Icon: Lungs)
  3. Feeling Cold (Icon: Thermometer)

### Slide 4: Definition (Instinct)
- **Visual**: Animal Kingdom imagery (Migration, Hibernation).
- **Text**: "INSTINCT" (Big). "Not learned. Just known."
- **Task**: "Name an instinct in animals."

### Slide 5: Task 1 - Personalization
- **Visual**: A "Danger" triangle icon?
- **Text**: "TASK 1: YOUR FEARS"
- **Instruction**: "Discuss in pairs: What scares you? What scares your family?"

### Slide 6: Segue - STAGE 2
- **Background**: Black with Amber EKG line.
- **Text**: "STAGE 2: CHOOSE YOUR LEVEL" (Self-Selection).

### Slide 7-9: Text Selection (The "Tiered Text" Choice)
- **Slide 7**: "LEVEL 1 (B1)" - Show a snippet (large font). "Comfortable?"
- **Slide 8**: "LEVEL 2 (B1+)" - Show paragraph snippet. "Challenging?"
- **Slide 9**: "LEVEL 3 (B2)" - Show paragraph snippet. "Expert?"
- **Action**: "Choose your challenge."

### Slide 10: Segue - STAGE 3
- **Background**: Maroon.
- **Text**: "STAGE 3: VOCABULARY WEAPONS" (Pre-teaching).

### Slides 11-15: Vocabulary (Standard Bell Format)
- **Format**: Word (Top) | Phonemic + Thai (Sub) | Context Sentences (Body).
- **Words**:
  1. **Instinct** (Image: Baby breathing/Bird flying)
  2. **Hormones** (Image: chemical structure/brain connection)
  3. **Adrenaline** (Image: Sprinter/Rollercoaster)
  4. **Glucose** (Image: Sugar/Muscles)
  5. **Organs** (Image: Medical scan of heart/lungs)
- **Audio Feature**: "Listen!" button for each word.

### Slide 16: Segue - STAGE 4
- **Background**: Amber (`#FFC107`). Dark Text? Or Black with Amber text.
- **Text**: "STAGE 4: DEEP DIVE" (Reading for Detail).

### Slide 17: Task 2 Instructions (Scanning)
- **Visual**: A "Search/Scan" radar icon.
- **Text**: "TASK 2: SCAN THE TEXT"
- **Instruction**: "Find the paragraphs. 6 Minutes. GO."

### Slide 18-20: Task 2 Answers (Interleaved)
- **Visual**: "Correct!" Green checks? Or stay on theme (Maroon/White).
- **Answers**: Text snippet highlighting the proof.
  - "1. Para 4", "2. Para 6", etc.

### Slide 21: Task 3 Instructions (Vocab Match)
- **Visual**: Puzzle piece icon.
- **Text**: "TASK 3: MATCH THE MEANING"
- **Instruction**: "Use the context. Find the definition."

### Slide 22: Task 3 Answers
- **Format**: List revealed one by one.
  - "1. self-defense"
  - "2. fight or flight instinct"
  - ... "6. organs"

### Slide 23: Segue - STAGE 5
- **Background**: White (Shock/Contrast) or Deep Grey.
- **Text**: "STAGE 5: CRITICAL THINKING" (Writing).

### Slide 24: Task 4 - The Writing Challenge
- **Visual**: Pen/Paper icon or "Thought Bubble".
- **Text**: "CHOOSE ONE QUESTION"
  1. "Is avoiding risk good or bad?"
  2. "Does fear stop you achieving goals?"
- **Constraint**: "70 Words. Use 'Adrenaline' and 'Instinct'."
- **Timer**: Embedded 7 Minute timer?

### Slide 25: Closing
- **Visual**: "Mission Complete" style.
- **Text**: "Great Work."
